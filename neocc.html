NEOCC Intranet Site

Back to projects

Client: Northeast Ohio Cyber Consortium.

Skills used: Microsoft SharePoint, HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

The Northeast Ohio Cyber Consortium was in need of a intranet in order to streamline and enhance communications within the organization. In this project, I worked in a team of students on a contract to develop a highly customized solution using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - as well as some built-in SharePoint CMS assets. We held multiple testing sessions in focus groups to collect user feedback and integrate that into the design.
